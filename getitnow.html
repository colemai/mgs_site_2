Screening Quiz

Take the Screening Quiz now to learn the type of IBS that you might be suffering from. Button to say “Start Quiz Here” The screening quiz has been designed to minimise risks associated with self-managing IBS. It was created following the guidelines outlined in the; HSE Cancer Guidelines; Ovarian Cancer Referral Guidelines. NICE Suspected cancer: recognition and referral. The Rome Foundation, Rome IV. Thus, you will be asked a series of questions about your symptoms. Remember, this is a screening tool only. It does not replace the relationship with your healthcare practice or service. It does not have any diagnostic capability. My Gut Solution strongly recommends that your medical doctor provide you with an IBS diagnosis before engaging with our solution.
